Candi Borobudur

Pengertian

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes with serrateed knife, approximately 6mm.
  2. Keep in water to prevent browning.
  3. Rinse with colander to remove excess sugars on the surface of potato.
  4. Simmer in vinegar water = superior crispiness
  5. Spread potato on tissue, no need to pat them dry, the residual heat will do our job
  6. Fry in 3 batches, with first two intervals in 10 seconds after dropping the batch, then 50 seconds for the last batch
  7. Drain and dry on tissue to remove excess oil
  8. Fry for the second time, with higher heat temperature
  9. Season it and lastly, serve it piping hot with dipping sauce!

Serve it piping hot makes the crispiness skyrocket, you wont even bother buying french fries from restaurants anymore!
